**How to use App Language:**

- Go to the profile icon on the top right side of the screen .
- Click on it and select " your profile".
- Choose the language you want to work in under the App language option.
You have option to choose from:
  - English
  - Hindi
  - Russian
  - Farsi
  - French
  - Nepali

- Click on "Save" button.
You can begin working in the language of your preference.
